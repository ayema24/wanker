Top five: flaws in popular hit series.

Emmanuel shogo

march 5th,2020.

5:25pm






I'm jobless for the time being.made up this crappy article that no one will read.i won't do all those long intros let's get straight to the point. I'll be highlighting five hit series and their flaws. Since obviously no series is impervious to errors and plot imbalances.












  • 1. INTO THE BADLANDS (2015-2019).

    Virtually every fan of good action and Kung Fu would have seen amc's into the badlands. Without doubts this show tops any other modern action series when it comes to the fight scenes.the choreography is simply outstanding.kudos! To the team.


    But like every series we will be checking out in this article,Into The badlands has it's flaws ranging from the imbalance of characters abilities and production dates.

    Season 1 of the series saw mk(Aramis Knight) as an unstoppable force, basically no one could stop him

    His punches were out of this world but the producers some how manages to reduce his strength in following seasons.The widow was easily overpowered by sunny and she returned the favour Season 3.hell, with all the feats sunny had accomplished a broken leg wasn't an excuse for getting beat by the widow.

    And if I'm not wrong the dark eyed ones were made useless than required. Only the main characters with dark eyes stood out. The rest were useless.

    Finally, let's not forget the imbalances in the release dates of episodes keeping viewers in unnecessary suspense.
